Welcome to Hallownest

Welcome to Hallownest, a vast, ruined kingdom full of secrets, danger, and unforgettable battles. Hollow Knight is renowned not just for its beautiful, hand-drawn world and haunting soundtrack, but also for its intense and memorable boss fights. Whether you're a first-time player struggling to survive your encounter with Hornet or a veteran prepping for the brutal Pantheon of Hallownest, this site is your companion through every nail-swinging, soul-draining battle the game throws at you. This guide was built with one purpose: to help you defeat every boss in Hollow Knight, no matter your skill level. From the early game’s deceptively simple enemies to the late-game challenges that push even the best players to their limits, we’ve broken down each boss with clear, detailed strategies. You’ll find recommended charms, movement tactics, attack patterns, and expert tips tailored to help you adapt and conquer. But this isn’t just about winning fights — it’s about understanding them. Each boss in Hollow Knight has its own rhythm, personality, and lore. By studying their behavior and mastering your own movement, you won’t just survive — you’ll thrive. And for those aiming to complete the Pantheons or pursue 112% completion, we’ve got advanced strategies and charm setups to help you optimize your build and approach.

On this site, you'll find:

  • And much more!
